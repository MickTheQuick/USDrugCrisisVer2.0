The Top Opioid Affected States: Illinois


Overdose Deaths

In 2015, the state of Illinois had a recorded 1,835 overdoses that resulted in death. A wallowing 75% (1,381) of those deaths were caused by opioid overdoses.

Chicago


Between 2013 and 2015 the city of Chicago saw approximately 1,425 deaths due to heroin (a type of opioid) overdoses alone which is a large portion in the 2,133 statewide.


East St. Louis

In 2015, East St. Louis had a recorded 889 violent crimes including 19 murders, 42 cases of rape, 146 robberies and 682 aggravated assaults.


Rockford

In 2015 Rockford recorded 2,349 violent crimes. There were 19 murders, 148 cases of rape, 504 robberies and 1,678 aggravated assaults.
