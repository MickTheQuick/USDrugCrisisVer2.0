The Top Opioid Affected States: Florida


Overdose Deaths

In 2015, the state of Florida had a recorded 3,228 overdoses that resulted in death. 57% (1,838) of those deaths were caused by opioid overdoses.

Palm Beach County

More than 375 people have died due to opioid overdoses between January and September of this year. This year's opioid deaths have already surpassed last year's total overdose deaths in the county.


Lake Worth

The city of Lake Worth has recorded that 75 people have died of suspected drug overdoses this year, that's a 56% increase from that of last year. It is suspected that 20 out of every 10,000 people have died of an overdose in the city.


West Palm Beach

The city of West Palm Beach has recorded that 70 people have died from suspected overdoses this year, six deaths shy of last year's total. It is suspected that 7 out of every 10,000 people have died due to overdoses in the city.
