The Top Opioid Affected States: North Carolina


Overdose Deaths

In 2015, the state of North Carolina had a recorded 1,567 overdoses that resulted in death. A wallowing 75% (1,171) of those deaths were caused by opioid overdoses.

Jacksonville

Jacksonville has a population of around 70,000 and 8.2% of all people with opioid prescriptions are abusing them. The high rate og the the drug use could be associated with the big military presence in the town. The veterans in the town are 10 times more likely to use the drugs than regular civilians.


Hickory

In Hickory, 9.9% of people with an opioid prescription are abusing the drug and 46.1% of all prescription drugs are being abused.


Wilmington

In Wilmington, more thatn 11.6% of people with an opioid prescription are abusing the drug. On top of that, 54% of prescriptions in the city are abused.
