The Top Opioid Affected States: Texas


Overdose Deaths

In 2015, the state of Ohio had a recorded 2,588 overdoses that resulted in death. Half (1,287) of those deaths were caused by opioid overdoses.

Lufkin

Out of the 35,000 residents in Lufkin, over 4,000 are suspected 'meth heads'. The meth problem is so bad that they had to created a 'Meth Watch' which is where people can watch those 'meth heads' on meth. Lufkin ranks in the top 10% of residents on welfare, the most amount of high school dropouts, and a high number of payday loans per capita.


Texarkana

Of the 39,000 residents in Texarkana, close to 5,000 are suspected to have an addiction to meth. It also ranks as top state in the number of aggravated assaults.


Dallas

Of the 1.3 million residents of Dallas, over 5,000 of them are suspected meth addicts. Dallas also has high numbers in assaults, drug use and other violent crimes.
